WHO ARE BTS?

BTS are a South Korean boy band made up of 7 members. They debuted in June 2013 as the only artist signed under Bighit Entertainment, which was a relatively small company at the time (The company is now worth $57 million USD.) The 7 members, from youngest to oldest, are known as Jungkook (21), V (23), Jimin (23), RM (24), J-Hope (25), Suga (26) and Jin (26). BTS stands for Beyond the Scene and their fandom is knowns as the ARMY.
